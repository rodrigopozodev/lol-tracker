# League of Legends Tracker

**League Tracker** is a comprehensive web application designed to help League of Legends players analyze their performance, track their progress, and improve their gameplay through detailed statistics and insights.

## How It Works

1. **Search**: Enter any player's Riot ID (GameName#TAG) to instantly access their profile
2. **Analyze**: View comprehensive statistics including rank, match history, and champion mastery
3. **Compare**: Add multiple players to compare their performance side by side
4. **Track**: Create an account to save your searches and track your own progress over time
5. **Improve**: Use the insights and statistics to identify areas for improvement in your gameplay

**League Tracker** transforms raw game data into meaningful insights, helping League of Legends players at all skill levels understand their performance and achieve their competitive goals.
