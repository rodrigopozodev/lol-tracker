## What League Tracker Does.

###  **Multi-Player Search & Comparison**

Search for multiple League of Legends players simultaneously and compare their statistics side by side. Whether you want to analyze your team's performance or scout opponents, you can look up players from any region and get instant access to their current rankings, match history, and champion preferences.

###  **Detailed Player Analytics**

Get comprehensive insights into any player's performance including:

- **Current Rankings**: View Solo Queue and Flex Queue ranks with exact LP (League Points)
- **Match History**: Analyze recent games with detailed statistics including KDA, CS, damage dealt, and game outcomes
- **Champion Mastery**: See which champions a player has mastered and their proficiency levels
- **Performance Trends**: Track wins, losses, and overall performance patterns

###  **Rank Tracking & Progress Monitoring**

Monitor your own or other players' ranked progression over time. The system tracks:

- Solo Queue and Flex Queue rankings
- LP gains and losses
- Tier and division changes
- Win/loss streaks and patterns

###  **Champion Performance Analysis**

Discover detailed champion-specific statistics:

- Most played champions with win rates
- Champion mastery points and levels
- Performance metrics per champion
- Strengths and areas for improvement

###  **Global Region Support**

Access player data from all League of Legends regions including:

- Europe West (EUW) and Europe Nordic & East (EUNE)
- North America (NA)
- Korea (KR)
- Brazil (BR)
- Latin America North & South (LAN/LAS)
- Japan (JP), Oceania (OCE), Russia (RU), and Turkey (TR)

###  **Personal Profile & Account Management**

Create your personal account to:

- Link your Riot Games account for automatic data updates
- Save favorite players for quick access
- Track your own progress over time
- Receive personalized insights and recommendations

###  **Performance Insights & Recommendations**

The application provides intelligent analysis to help players improve:

- Identify strengths and weaknesses in gameplay
- Compare performance against players of similar rank
- Discover optimal champion picks based on current meta
- Track improvement over time with visual progress indicators
